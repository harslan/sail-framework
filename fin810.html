01 ‚Äî The Problem

Your Brain on ChatGPT

In June 2025, researchers at MIT's Media Lab published a study that should concern every finance student. Dr. Nataliya Kosmyna and her team fitted 54 participants with EEG headsets and asked them to write essays ‚Äî some with AI assistance, some with search engines, and some using only their own knowledge.

The results were stark. Participants who relied on AI showed the weakest neural connectivity ‚Äî their brains were literally less engaged. The search-engine group fared moderately. The brain-only group showed the strongest connections. Worse, the AI-assisted group reported the lowest sense of ownership over their own work and struggled to recall what they had written.

"There is no cognitive credit card. You cannot pay this debt off." ‚Äî Dr. Nataliya Kosmyna, MIT Media Lab, June 2025

The researchers named this phenomenon cognitive debt ‚Äî the hidden cost of outsourcing your thinking to a machine. Unlike financial debt, there's no repayment plan. Once you've trained yourself to let AI think for you, the neural pathways that build judgment, memory, and analytical skill begin to atrophy.

In investment analysis, this isn't an abstract concern. It's the difference between getting the job and freezing when the interviewer says, "Walk me through your valuation."

04 ‚Äî The AIChemist Challenge

Think First. Augment Second. Defend Always.

The Investment AIChemist Challenge is the core experience of this course. You'll analyze a real company through three phases ‚Äî each designed to build a different layer of analytical skill.

1

Think First ‚Äî 45 minutes, no AI

You develop your own investment thesis using only your knowledge and provided financial data. No AI tools. No shortcuts. This is where judgment forms ‚Äî your initial read on a company's fundamentals, risks, and valuation before any machine touches it. This phase builds the neural pathways that prevent cognitive debt.

2

AI Augmentation ‚Äî 30 minutes, strategic AI use

Now you use AI ‚Äî but strategically. Challenge your own assumptions. Stress-test your model. Look for blind spots you missed. Every AI-generated insight must be verified against primary sources. You document exactly what AI contributed and what you kept, changed, or rejected. A Transparency Appendix tracks it all.

3

Defense ‚Äî live Q&A from peers and instructor

Present your analysis and defend it under questioning. Why did you choose that discount rate? What happens if subscriber growth slows by 20%? Where exactly did this data come from? This simulates the real-world pressure of client meetings, investment committees, and job interviews. If you can't defend it, it isn't yours.

The analyst who can explain every assumption and handle pushback is the one who gets the offer. FIN 810 makes sure that analyst is you.
05 ‚Äî The SAIL Framework

Four Pillars Against Cognitive Debt

SAIL is the pedagogical framework behind every assignment in FIN 810. Each pillar targets a specific type of cognitive debt identified in the MIT research ‚Äî ensuring you build genuine capability, not just AI-assisted output.

S

Social Intelligence

Defend your analysis to peers who will challenge every assumption. Practice the interpersonal skills of presenting investment theses, handling objections, and building conviction through dialogue ‚Äî skills AI cannot perform for you.

Prevents ‚Üí Ownership debt & Defense debt
A

AI Literacy

Understand what AI actually does well (pattern recognition, data synthesis) and where it fails (hallucinations, fabricated sources, overconfident claims). The "Catching AI" workshop trains you to identify errors before they reach a client.

Prevents ‚Üí Verification debt & Judgment debt
I

Innovation & Inquiry

Develop your own investment thesis before consulting AI. Think first. The StockTrak journal and weekly reflections build the habit of original analysis ‚Äî ensuring AI augments your thinking rather than replacing it.

Prevents ‚Üí Memory debt & Vocabulary debt
L

Leadership

Take ownership of your analytical process. Document how and when you used AI. Maintain a transparency log. In a profession governed by CFA ethics standards, the ability to account for your process isn't optional ‚Äî it's required.

Prevents ‚Üí Transparency debt
